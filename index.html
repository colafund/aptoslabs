About Us

AptosYo is an NFT Avatars composed of 10,000 PFP-NFT sets and living on the Aptos blockchain. It is a unique, scalable and fused NFT.

At the end of the 21st century, economic recession, energy crisis, etc. have caused mankind to lose their homeland for survival. It is urgent to find extraterrestrial civilization. Extraterrestrial exploration will start from Ushuaia, the end of the world, and build its own ecology in the process of exploration— -Kunta Ecology, each AptosYo is also the identity card of Kunta Ecology, and enjoy the exclusive entrance of Kunta Ecology, participate in construction, participate in play and earn, unlock and explore unknown areas together .
